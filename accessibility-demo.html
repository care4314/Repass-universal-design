summarize Overview

Rocky Mountain National Park (RMNP) is a United States national park in north-central Colorado, about 55 miles (89 km) northwest of Denver, situated between Estes Park (east) and Grand Lake (west). The Continental Divide runs through the park; headwaters of the Colorado River arise in the park’s northwest. Landscapes range from forest to alpine tundra, with mountains, lakes, and diverse wildlife.
